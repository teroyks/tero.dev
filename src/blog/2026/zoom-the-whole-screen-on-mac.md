A really cool tip on [Unsung](https://unsung.aresluna.org/testing-tip-enable-the-zoom-peek-gesture): You can quickly zoom in on any part of your screen with the scroll gesture (on a trackpad or a mouse), holding down the Control key (or any other modifier you set), by enabling an accessibility setting.

- Turn on _Settings → Accessibility → Zoom → Use scroll gesture with modifier keys to zoom_.
- Turn off _Advanced… → Smooth images_.
- Zoom away!
