... or, at least, I've been told [TRON: Ares](https://www.themoviedb.org/movie/533533-tron-ares) is pretty terrible. I haven't dared watch it (I loved the original TRON as a kid), but maybe I'll still get to it one of these days to see for myself.

But that is beside the point: [the soundtrack](https://album.link/t/460456659) for the movie is by Nine Inch Nails, and I like it a lot. It gives you the feeling of a dark, digital world, and is very good music to code to. The just-released [TRON Ares: Divergence](https://album.link/i/1880537172) is a fine remix with added techno vibes.

(I also loved Daft Punk's [soundtrack for TRON: Legacy](https://album.link/i/1543824814). Seems the keepers of the franchise are better at hiring great musicians for the score than making the actual movies.)
